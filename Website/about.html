about

staples commercial group


Since 2017, our brokerage, Staples Commercial Group, has been proudly serving the East Texas real estate market. Our team expanded to include agents with expertise in commercial real estate. This growth led to the division solely dedicated to commercial real estate investments.

With over 50 years of combined real estate experience, Ray Ver Hey, Steve Demuth, Jarrod Ver Hey, and Kerri Cole Jordon form a dedicated and hard-working team. We prioritize our clients' needs and strive to deliver exceptional service in every transaction.

What sets Staples Commercial Group apart is our superior marketing strategy. We go above and beyond to promote our listings, ensuring maximum visibility for our clients. We utilize renowned platforms such as CoStar, LoopNet, Crexi, Catalyst, and prominent commercial listing services nationwide, including the Dallas/Ft Worth, Houston, and Austin MLS's.

Our extensive reach ensures that our clients' properties are showcased to a wide range of potential buyers, sellers, landlords, and tenants. Moreover, our deep understanding and knowledge of the East Texas market provide significant value to our clients.

Whether you're a buyer, seller, landlord, or tenant, our expertise enables us to offer valuable insights and guidance throughout the entire real estate process. When it comes to commercial real estate in East Texas, look no further than Staples Commercial Group. We are here to meet all your commercial real estate needs with professionalism, dedication, and a proven track record of success.
